How to Choose The Right Makeup for Your Skin Type?

It can be tough to find the perfect product for your skin that looks good at work and after hours. Fret not; here are some guidelines to make your online makeup purchases with ease.

  • IDENTIFY YOUR SKIN TYPE:
    Oily, dry, combination, and sensitive skin are the four main categories when it comes to your face. Understand your type and select makeup cosmetics that complement it. For instance, go for a matte foundation for oily skin and a creamy one for dry.
  • UNDERSTAND THE SKIN SHADES:
    From milky fair to warm and cool undertones and even dusky skin, there are so many varieties of beautiful faces in our country. Hence, we offer products that suit almost all shades. Identify your shade and opt for nude or pop hues as per your choice.
  • BE EVENT READY:
    Ensure that your makeup online shopping is versatile and ideal for most occasions. For instance, you can pick an eyeshadow palette with a mix-bag of shades for weddings as well as office parties. You can even buy tester kits on our platform.
  • ADDRESS YOUR SKIN ISSUES:
    If you are prone to acne, look for products that are light and matte. Alternately, choose correcting and concealing shades for pigmented skin type. Plus, you can also look for items that are hypoallergenic and fragrance-free if you have sensitive skin. Ensure that you wash your makeup brushes often to avoid infections.

WHAT ARE MAKEUP COMBOS?

Makeup combos are collections of cosmetics that are offered as a bundle deal. Different items such as primer, foundation, and compact could be one set, or color-based options such as lip and cheek tint, eye shadows, and bold-hued eyeliners. You may even go for an eye-makeup combo which comprises liner, kajal, eyebrow pigment, shadow, mascara, and more. These sets are frequently packaged and can be more affordable than purchasing separate products.
